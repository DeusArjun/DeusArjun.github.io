About Us

We are a community of Irvington students who enjoy answering questions about a variety of topics. We are free to join, open to all, and maintain a fun and relaxed practice environment.

We also attend lots of tournaments. Our members have won at regional tournaments and have gone as far as the National level in Quiz Bowl, History Bowl, and Geography championships.
